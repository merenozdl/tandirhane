Who Are We?

Tandırhane is a family-owned restaurant that blends the essence of traditional Turkish cuisine with modern presentations. Established in 2025, our restaurant is dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of Anatolia.

All our dishes are cooked over a wood fire using fresh and natural ingredients. We offer our guests a warm, nostalgic atmosphere and authentic flavors. Our specialty tandır oven creates unforgettable experiences through traditional recipes.

With our philosophy of "Real flavor comes with patience," we invite you to join our tables and experience true Turkish hospitality.
